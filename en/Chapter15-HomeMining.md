###### footnotes and quotes

I want to teach people what mining is with visual aids like hexadecimal dice I found. The idea is to use photographs to visually teach the mehanics of mining. I find the leading hex zeros in programs like [hashcash](https://hashcash.org) facinating. My hypothesis is that hexidecimal dice will make this concept of timestamping transactions something to relate it to. Other authors like Kiara Brickle have used dice as an analogy to describe bitcoin mining. I am doing the same thing with 16-sided dice instead of 6 sided dice. 11=A and so on and so fourth. This represents the hash in the block header better.
